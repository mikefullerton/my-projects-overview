## Ageism Strategy

### The Reframe
- **Salaried IC:** age is a headwind. Companies prefer mid-career engineers they can shape.
- **Consulting at this level:** age is an ASSET. Clients paying $5k/day want scars, judgment, and pattern-matching across multiple platform shifts. "35 years" is a feature.
- **Key requirement:** must show currency, not just legacy. Claude Code / agentic expertise solves this. "35 years in software AND on the frontier of agentic development" is the rare combination.
- **Practical:** consulting positions you against other advisors (experience compounds). Salaried positions you against younger engineers (ageism bites).
